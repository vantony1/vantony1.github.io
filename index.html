Victor Nikhil Antony


I am a Ph.D. student at the Johns Hopkins University advised by Dr. Chien-Ming Huang. I work on designing, building and evaulating social robots. I am interested in understanding how social robots can support well-being in longitudinal and in-the-wild settings. Prior to joining Hopkins, I was a undergraduate student at the University of Rochester supervised by Dr. Ehsan Hoque and Dr. Zhen Bai.

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to me via mail!
